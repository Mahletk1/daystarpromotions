About Daystar Promotions...

I began booking concerts for Michael in 1999. At first, just to help him out, it was a ministry for me, thus began DayStar Promotions. For the first few years I didn't charge Michael for doing this, but my financial situation changed and when Michael learned of that, he insisted on paying me.

Over the years I have helped several artists with their booking such as Russ Taff, Steve Ladd, Wes Hampton of the Gaither Vocal Band, Melissa Greene, Jody McBrayer both formerly with Avalon, Jonathan Martin of The Martins, Jaime Thietten. Shane Dunlap formerly with Signature Sound, Biney English Family and N'Harmony, to name a few. I'm thankful and proud to have been a small part of these ministries.

I know God gave me this business and soon after my motto became "More Than a Concert" because Michael's c oncerts offer so much more than a just a concert or entertainment, always their testimonies, sharing God's word, and always hoping lives will be changed or blessed by what God does at their concerts.

I love what I do and the people that I have worked with and I still feel this is a ministry for me. As I can play a small part by getting their dates booked, where they can share their stories of God's grace, redemption and restoration in their lives.

Ask me about booking Michael in concert, it may be easier than you think!

                                       ~ Reneé
